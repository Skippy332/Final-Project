Constitution

Faith Baptist Church is not organizationally connected to a denomination or association of churches. We seek to align every aspect of our ministry to the Word of God: our worship, our preaching, our doctrine, and our lives. Our church constitution reflects our attempt to implement New Testament church life into our congregation. We have a church constitution because we believe that the operation of the church should be transparent and orderly. But while this document is important to us we are first and foremost committed to holding the Bible as our sole authority for faith and practice.
